Welcome to Run169

We are a service that provides insights and tools to the members of the Run169Towns Society. To sign up for this website, you will need to make an account there first since that is the official database for running the 169 towns. This website makes it easier for you to submit towns, share your results, and has more tools for those data-nerds out there. We are in no way associated to the official Run169Towns website.

Features

  • Easily submit towns to Run169Society
  • Race Calendars
  • Upcoming Races from RunSignup
  • Runner Profiles
  • Race Results and Ranks within 169
  • Analysis on your progress and races
